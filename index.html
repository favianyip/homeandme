ABOUT US

Renovation, made honest

Home & Me began with a simple frustration: renovating in Singapore meant juggling quotes you couldn't compare, prices that moved after you signed, and designs you couldn't see until they were built.

So we put the entire journey online — curated design themes, photoreal renders of your actual selections, itemised live pricing and vetted contractors — so every homeowner can complete their home with confidence.
