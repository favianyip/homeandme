Privacy and legal

Home & Me collects only the information needed to set up and manage your renovation project — your contact details, property information and design selections.

Your data is never sold to third parties. It is shared with our vetted contractors and partners only to the extent required to carry out your renovation.

You may request a copy of your data, or ask for it to be deleted, at any time via the contact form.

Terms of service

Prices shown while designing are live, itemised estimates. Your final quote is confirmed at checkout and honoured for the works specified in it.

Project timelines are indicative and may vary with site conditions, material availability and regulatory approvals.

By checking out you appoint Home & Me to coordinate the specified works through our vetted contractors and partners, with payments released by milestone.

Cookie policy

We use essential cookies to keep your project session alive while you design, and analytics cookies to understand how the platform is used.

No advertising or cross-site tracking cookies are set. You can clear cookies at any time in your browser without losing a saved project.

Quick Fixes

Not every job is a full renovation. Quick Fixes covers smaller works — a leaking tap, new shelving, repainting a room, replacing tiles or fixtures.

Describe the job through the contact form and we'll match you with a vetted handyman or specialist, with the same transparent, agreed-upfront pricing.

For partners

Home & Me works with a curated network of contractors, carpenters and material suppliers who share our standard of transparent pricing and reliable delivery.

Partners receive scoped, itemised job orders through the platform — no quoting wars, no chasing payments.

Interested in joining the network? Reach out and our partnerships team will be in touch.
