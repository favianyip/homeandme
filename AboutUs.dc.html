ABOUT US

Renovation, made honest

Home & Me is Singapore's online renovation studio — design every room, tour photoreal renders, and check out with one itemised, transparent price.

We started with a simple frustration

Renovating in Singapore meant juggling quotes you couldn't compare, prices that moved after you signed, and designs you couldn't see until they were built. Homeowners were expected to commit tens of thousands of dollars on faith.

So we put the entire journey online. Configure your home in under a minute. Explore curated design themes specified down to the last tile. Walk through photoreal renders of your actual selections. And before you pay a cent, see every line item — hacking, masonry, carpentry, plumbing, finishes — priced live.

The work itself is carried out by vetted contractors and partners, assigned and managed through the platform, with every milestone tracked from deposit to handover.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

COST TRANSPARENCY

Every scope itemised and priced live. The total at checkout is the total you pay — no surprises mid-project.

DESIGN YOU CAN SEE

Photoreal renders of your exact selections — not mood boards — so you know what you're building before it's built.

ACCOUNTABLE DELIVERY

Vetted contractors, milestones tracked online, and one team answerable for the whole project.
